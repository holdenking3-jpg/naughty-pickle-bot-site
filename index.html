Common questions.

Will callers know they're talking to an AI? +

Our agents use natural, conversational voices that are nearly indistinguishable from human reps. That said, we always recommend transparency — we can configure your agent to disclose it's AI if required or preferred in your industry.

What happens if the agent can't answer a question? +

Your agent is trained with a fallback protocol — it can gracefully transfer calls to a human, take a message, or schedule a callback. We design for edge cases during the build phase so nothing falls through the cracks.

How long does setup actually take? +

Most clients go live within 48–72 hours of their discovery call. Complex multi-agent builds may take up to 5 business days. We'll give you a firm timeline before you pay anything.

Can I update the agent's script or behavior later? +

Yes — your monthly plan includes ongoing tune-ups. Just email us what you want changed and we'll handle it, usually within 24 hours. Growth clients get priority turnaround and can request changes via Slack.

Is there a contract or commitment? +

No long-term contracts. You pay the setup fee once, then month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We're confident in our results — we don't need to lock you in.
